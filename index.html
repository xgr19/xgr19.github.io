1   Introduction

My name is Guorui Xie (谢国锐). In 2019, I received a bachelor's degree from Sun Yat-sen University (Guangzhou). Now I am a PhD student in Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University (Shenzhen). My main research directions are in-network intelligence, P4 program development, and network traffic classification (or detection, prediction, etc.) based on deep learning.
