About Me

I'm Guorui Xie (谢国锐 in Chinese). I received my BEng (2019) and PhD (2024) degrees from SYSU and Tsinghua(China), respectively. I was also a CSC-funded research visitor at Polimi (Italy, 2023~2024).

Now I am an Assistant Researcher (PhD supervisor) at Pengcheng Laboratory(PCL). My research interests are: programmable network, P4. I have published 20+ papers in NSDI, INFOCOM, ICNP, ToN, etc.

2019年本科毕业于中山大学，2024年博士毕业于清华大学，曾获国家留学基金资助海外联培（2023~2024）、腾讯青云计划。目前是鹏城实验室（PCL）助理研究员、博导（清华智能网络团队），研究P4可编程网络技术等，已在NSDI、INFOCOM、ICNP、ToN等刊物发表论文20余篇。
