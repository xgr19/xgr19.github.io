About Me

My name is Guorui Xie (谢国锐 in Chinese). I received my BEng (2019) and PhD (2024) degrees from Sun Yat-sen University(China) and Tsinghua University(China), respectively. I was also a research visitor at Politecnico di Milano(Italy) in 2023-2024.

Currently, I am an Assistant Researcher at Pengcheng Laboratory(National Laboratory, China).

My main research interest is traffic analysis on programmable network devices, including 1) deploy P4 based ML/DL models for traffic analysis; 2) design the privacy-secured traffic obfuscation system; 3) traffic measurement and scheduling.
